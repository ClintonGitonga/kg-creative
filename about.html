Founder Story

The thinking behind the work — and the standard we deliver every time.

Kibuga Gitonga is the founder and creative director of KG Creative, a boutique studio in Nairobi specializing in photography, graphic design, and social media strategy.

With over a decade of hands-on experience, he blends creative intuition with disciplined strategic thinking. His work is defined by precision, clarity, and a deep understanding of how powerful visuals shape perception and drive growth.

KG Creative partners with a select roster of clients, from entrepreneurs to educational institutions, offering personalized attention and premium content tailored to each brand’s unique identity. Kibuga’s approach remains consistent across every project: intentional creativity, refined execution, and results that stand the test of time.
