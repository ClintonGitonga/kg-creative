About KG Creative

KG Creative is a professional photography and graphic design studio based in Nairobi, Kenya. We provide high-quality photography for weddings, corporate events, portraits, and social media branding.

Our mission is to create stunning visuals that capture memories and help brands stand out. Creativity, professionalism, and client satisfaction are our top priorities.
